Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

San Diego Lifestyle Guide (the Guide) is a locally owned editorial publication. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this solely to send the Guide's editorial content. We do not sell, share, or rent subscriber email addresses.

When you visit this site, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may log standard server-access data (IP address, browser type, referring URL, page visited, date/time). We do not have access to individual server logs and do not use this data for tracking.

Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use advertising trackers, behavioral targeting cookies, or third-party analytics pixels. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN; Google's privacy policy governs that connection. No other third-party cookies are set.

Newsletter

Our newsletter is delivered via Beehiiv. When you subscribe, your email address is stored in Beehiiv's platform. Beehiiv's privacy policy governs their handling of that data. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter.

Editorial Independence

San Diego Lifestyle Guide is editorially independent. Some articles contain links to local businesses marked as sponsored partnerships (rel="sponsored"); these are clearly identified. Sponsored links do not influence editorial coverage. We do not accept payment for editorial mentions.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email sdlifestyle@proton.me.
