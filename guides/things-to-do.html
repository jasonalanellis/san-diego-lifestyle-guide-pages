Things to Do in San Diego

San Diego earns its reputation across every category of experience — coastal hiking at Torrey Pines, off-leash beaches, 17 Balboa Park museums, the Zoo, a craft beer scene with genuine depth, and a nightlife district in the Gaslamp that stays lively well past midnight. Every neighborhood adds its own layer, and the day trips within an hour's drive are among the best in the West.
